This interactive map visualizes the global landscape of Christian persecution using data compiled from seven major international monitoring organizations.

Data Sources

Methodology

The primary ranking and scoring system is from the Open Doors World Watch List 2025, which evaluates persecution across six categories: Private Life, Family Life, Community Life, National Life, Church Life, and Violence. Each category is scored 0-16.7, for a maximum total of 100.

Additional designations from USCIRF, ACN, VOM, ICC, and HeartCry Missionary Society are overlaid to provide a comprehensive multi-source view.

Disclaimer

This map is for educational and awareness purposes. Data reflects the most recently available reports from each organization. Conditions change rapidly and this map may not reflect the current situation in every country.
